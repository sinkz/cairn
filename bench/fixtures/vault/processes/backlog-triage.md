# Context

Support and PO review blocked customer cases when the backlog has more urgent
items than the team can finish in the current cycle.

# Steps

1. Confirm the customer impact and severity.
2. Prioritize the backlog item against committed product work.
3. Record the owner, decision, and next update in the customer-facing ticket.
